Welcome to Algorithmic

Empowering Young Minds Through Computer Science Tutoring

About Us

At Algorithmic, we are passionate about empowering young minds through computer science education. Our tutoring services are specifically designed for students aged 9-12, but kids of all ages are welcome! We provide a fun and engaging way to learn coding and programming and as mentors, we use a personalized approach to help students develop their skills and achieve their full potential.

Meet the Founder

I am Sneha! I'm a rising junior at West Windsor-Plainsboro High School South with a strong background in computer science and tutoring. My coding expertise spans multiple languages, including Java, HTML/CSS, and Python. I'm excited to share my knowledge and help others excel in this field.

Our Mission

Our mission is empowering young minds through computer science, beginning in Plainsboro and expanding to schools everywhere. We want to make computer science accessible and fun! Through computer science, we aim to bridge gaps, break down barriers, and pave the way for a brighter and more equitable tomorrow.

Course Overview

Session 1: Python

Feb 11 - Mar 10

This session will mainly focus on Python and game development. Students will use Repl.it to write and store their code. Classes will be held every Sunday (10 am - 11 am) over Zoom. A total of 5 classes will be covered.

Session 2: HTML/CSS

May 19 - Jun 16

This session will mainly focus on HTML/CSS and web development. Students will use Repl.it to write and store their code. Classes will be held every Sunday (10 am - 11 am) over Zoom. A total of 5 classes will be covered.

Final Project

At the end of each session, students will complete a final project: developing a multi-level game using Python skills and creating a personal portfolio/blog using HTML/CSS.
